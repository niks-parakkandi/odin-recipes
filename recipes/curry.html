Indian Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry from India typically features chicken stewed in a tomato-based sauce seasoned with aromatic spices.

This recipe, like many others, calls for curry powder.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 bay leaf
  • grated fresh ginger root
  • teaspoon white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast, cubed
  • tomato paste
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • coconut milk
  • lemon juice
  • cayenne pepper
